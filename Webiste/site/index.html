Restaurant Tranan has existed since 1955 and is still running!


Make a visit in the towns oldest, freshest and most luxurious restaurant. We provide the greatest and tastiest food that you wont be able to find anywhere else. Our beverages and range of different wines are enormous and highest of quality. All this ensures that you have the best experience here at Tranan that you wont forget or regret!


Restaurant Tranan, founded by Roger Edvardsson Trump, 1954 at Stockholms gallery in the streets of Iversenvagen 14. Started its business in 1955 with a less successful year. The year after the business went fluent and Tranan got a lot of donations to keep running everything. Year 1966 a couple got married at this place, the queen and king of Sweden. Since then the restaurant has been known for its tributing to new couples and marriages!
